InnerRailToggle

Current Version: 3.0.6-pre
Related Components: ShellWidget, Page, InnerRail
Github: coralui-component-shell

Defines a widget that reflects the selected state of an inner rail panel. The target inner rail is specified by the data-target attribute.

Note that by itself, the InnerRailToggle will not show or hide a panel on being clicked. For that to happen an instance of CUI.Page must be loaded too.
