CoralUI provides basic text styles suitable for paragraphs.

Fonts and Weights

The following table demonstrates the different font familes available in CoralUI by default. They are shown in different styles and weights.

font-family font-weight font-style sample

adobe-clean 300 normal The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy sleeping dog.
adobe-clean 300 italic The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy sleeping dog.
adobe-clean-condensed normal normal The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy sleeping dog.
adobe-clean-condensed bold none The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy sleeping dog.
