InnerRail

Current Version: 3.0.6-pre
Related Components: ShellWidget, Page, BreadcrumbBar
Github: coralui-component-shell

The inner rail shows on the left-hand side of the main page, and on the right hand side of the outer-rail. When horizontal space is limited, the inner and outer rail may not be shown simultaniously.
