Heading

Current Version: 2.3.8-pre

Related Components:

Headings require a coral-Heading selector, plus a coral-Heading--<LEVEL> selector, where <LEVEL> is the heading level desired.

The heading level selectors are:

.coral-Heading--1
.coral-Heading--2
.coral-Heading--3
.coral-Heading--4
.coral-Heading--5
.coral-Heading--6
